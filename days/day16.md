二、常用表达法

1、句子
(1) Is this the right way to the zoo?这是去公园的路吗？
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-01.mp3" controls="true"></audio>

<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-02.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(3) Could you tell me how to get to the zoo?你能告诉我怎样才能到动物园吗？
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-03.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(4) Is it far from here?它离这里远吗？
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-04.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(5) I’ve come the wrong way.我走错路了。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-05.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(6) Let’s transfer at the next stop.我们到下一站换车吧。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-06.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(7) This is the shortest way.这是最近的路。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-07.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(8) Where is the ticket office?售票处在哪里？
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-08.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(9) You must transfer at the next bus stop.你必须在下一站换车。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-09.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(10) That bus is packed full.那辆公共汽车挤的满满的了。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-10.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(11) Let’s rush there by taxi.我们坐出租车去那吧。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-11.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(12) What’s the fare to the station?去车站车费多少？
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-12.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(13) You can’t park here.此处不能停车。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-13.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(14) It’s over there.就在那里。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-14.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(15) It’s just around the corner.就在大街的拐角附近。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-15.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(16) It’s opposite the post office.就在邮局对面。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-16.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(17) It’s next to the department store.就在百货商店隔壁。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-17.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(18) It’s on the third floor.在三楼。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-18.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(19) It’s only a twenty-minutes’ walk from here.走20分钟就到。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-19.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(20) Go straight ahead about 300 meters.朝前一直走约300米。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-20.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(21) Go straight ahead until you come to a wide street, then turn left.沿这条路一直朝前走，到一条宽街时，向左转。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-21.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(22) Go that way about a hundred meters. When you come to a lane turn right.沿这条街走约100米，当你看到一条小巷向右转。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-22.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(23) Keep going until you see a big white building on your left. The place you’re looking for is right next to it.一直朝前走，直到你看见左边一幢白色的大楼，你要找的地方就在旁边。
<audio src="/audio/class16/16-02-01-23.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(24) Take the No.6 bus to the station. After you get off walk back about a hundred meters or so.乘6路公共汽车到车站，下车后，往回走100米左右。
