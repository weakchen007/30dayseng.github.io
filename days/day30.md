## 二、常用表达法

### 1、句子

(1) Can you contact me with London, please?你能帮我接通伦敦吗？
<audio src="/audio/class30/30-02-01.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(2) Can you put me through to 23547?能帮我转接23547吗？
<audio src="/audio/class30/30-02-02.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(3) Central! I’ve been cut off.总机！电话突然中断了。
<audio src="/audio/class30/30-02-03.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(4) Connect me with Queen Hotel. Please.请帮我接通皇后宾馆。
<audio src="/audio/class30/30-02-04.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(5) Could I have extension 564?能帮我接通分机564吗？
<audio src="/audio/class30/30-02-05.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(6) A minute, please. I’ll connect you.请稍等一下，马上为您接通。
<audio src="/audio/class30/30-02-06.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(7) I’m sorry the number’s engaged. Will you hold?抱歉，电话占线，您愿意稍等吗？
<audio src="/audio/class30/30-02-07.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(8) Just a minute. I’ll get the number through.请稍等，我帮您接通号码。
<audio src="/audio/class30/30-02-08.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(9) Number, please.请问要拨打的号码是多少？
<audio src="/audio/class30/30-02-09.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(10) The line is busy. 电话占线。
<audio src="/audio/class30/30-02-10.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(11) The line’s through now. 电话现在已经接通。
<audio src="/audio/class30/30-02-11.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(12) Where’re you calling from? 您从哪里打电话来？
<audio src="/audio/class30/30-02-12.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(13) Who do you want to speak to?您想找哪位接电话？
<audio src="/audio/class30/30-02-13.mp3" controls="true"></audio>
(14) Hello, Is Bright in?喂喂，请问布莱特在吗？
